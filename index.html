About the Project

Welcome to the Bird Sounds of the Guadiana River project. This initiative aims to document and share the beautiful bird sounds recorded along the Guadiana River. Explore our interactive map to discover the locations of these recordings, and visit our recordings page for detailed information and audio clips of each bird species.
